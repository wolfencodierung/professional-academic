About the presenter: John Markovic was born in the former Yugoslavia (presently Serbia) and immigrated to the United States in 1969. He is married to Vesna, and together they have a son, John-Phillip, and a daughter, Christianna. Professor Markovic graduated with honors from Andrews University in 1982 with bachelor’s degrees in theology and history. He earned his MA/PhD at Bowling Green State University, Bowling Green, Ohio, in 1990. He has taught at Andrews since the fall of 1990 and has been doing research on the emerging/emergent church movement and Emergence Christianity since 2007.
